## About
This personal website is a showcase of my work, interests, and a bit about myself. It includes the following sections:

-**Home:** An introduction to what I do and the latest updates or highlights.
- **About Me:** A detailed introduction to who I am, my background, and my passions.
- **Portfolio:** A collection of my notable projects with descriptions and links.
- **Blog:** My personal blog where I share thoughts, insights, and experiences.
- **Contact:** A way to get in touch with me.

## Features
- Responsive design for a seamless experience on various devices.
- An interactive portfolio section with project details.
- A blog section for sharing my knowledge and experiences.
- A contact form for direct communication.

## Usage
To use this code for your personal website:

1. Clone the repository.
2. Customize the content in HTML and update the CSS to match your style.
3. Add your own projects and blog posts to the respective sections.
4. Host the website on your preferred platform.

Feel free to reach out to me if you have any questions or need assistance with customization.
